Selected Case Study

Financial Services — Network Latency Remediation

A global financial client experienced sustained latency that degraded trading and analytics workflows. I led the strategy end-to-end, orchestrating discovery across network, compute, and app teams to isolate root causes.

Problem

Legacy, inefficient paths and saturated ports added avoidable hops and queuing delay.

Approach

Mapped flows, baselined latency, and produced a clean reference architecture; drove director-level alignment and a governed change plan.

Role

Strategy lead & technical owner — facilitated teams, authored design, and oversaw cutover.

Read more (architecture deltas & partnerships)

Consolidated routes and removed redundant hops; standardized uplinks; right-sized port channels. Partnered with the client and OEMs, applying core Dell stacks across transport through application layers to streamline data paths.

Delivered an executive-ready deck quantifying latency impact and ROI, aligning stakeholders on the remediation plan.

Outcome

  • 15% end-to-end latency reduction
  • $200,000 annual savings
  • Director-level buy-in and cross-team adoption
