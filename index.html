Enterprise Success Story

Financial Services — Network Latency Remediation

A global financial client experienced sustained latency that degraded trading and analytics workflows. I led strategy and delivery, facilitating L1–L7 investigation across network, compute, and application teams to isolate the root causes.

Problem

Inefficient paths and saturated links added avoidable hops and queuing delay.

Approach

Mapped flows, baselined latency, authored a clean reference architecture; directed governed change plans with exec alignment.

Role

Lifecycle owner — stakeholder facilitation, design authority, cutover leadership.

Read more (architecture deltas & partnerships)

Consolidated routes and removed redundant hops; standardized uplinks; right-sized port channels. Partnered with client and OEMs, applying multi-vendor cloud + network stacks across transport through application layers to streamline data paths.

Delivered an executive brief quantifying latency impact and ROI, aligning stakeholders on remediation and post-implementation KPIs.

Outcome

  • 15% end-to-end latency reduction
  • $200,000 annual savings
  • Director-level buy-in and cross-team adoption
